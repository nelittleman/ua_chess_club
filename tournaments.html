About the Tournament

The Fall Rapid Chess Tournament is open to all University of Arizona students, staff, and alumni. Matches will follow a 15+10 time control with Swiss-style pairings. Prizes are awarded to the top three finishers.

Date: Saturday, November 15, 2025
Location: Student Union – Rincon Room
Format: 4-round Swiss | Time Control: 15+10
