Get Involved with the UA Wildcat Chess Club

There’s more to the game than moves — help us grow our chess community through service, leadership, and teamwork.

Volunteer Opportunities

Whether you’re a seasoned player or just love helping others learn, there are many ways to get involved:

  • Event Setup & Logistics: Assist with table setup, chess board arrangement, and check-in for tournaments.
  • Teaching & Mentorship: Help mentor new players, lead small-group tutorials, or offer strategy sessions.
  • Media & Outreach: Design flyers, manage social media, and spread awareness across campus.
  • Community Outreach: Partner with local schools or youth organizations to promote chess in the community.

Leadership Roles

Club officers are selected annually and help guide the mission and activities of the Wildcat Chess Club.

  • Event Coordinator: Organize tournaments and social gatherings.
  • Public Relations Officer: Manage social media and design promotional materials.
  • Volunteer Chair: Coordinate event volunteers and outreach efforts.

Interested in joining the leadership team? Elections are announced each spring. Stay tuned via our Events page for details.

Ready to Join the Action?

Sign up as a volunteer or express interest in future leadership roles — your contribution helps make the club stronger.
