About the UA Wildcat Chess Club

Building community, strategy, and lifelong friendships — one move at a time.

Our Mission

The Wildcat Chess Club's mission is to provide an inclusive space where people can learn about and play chess, both casually and competitively.

History

Founded in 2020, the Wildcat Chess Club (WCC) began as a small group of students meeting in the Student Union to share strategies and host informal matches. Today, we’ve grown into an official ASUA-recognized student organization with regular tournaments, lessons, and workshops open to all members.

Bylaws

All club operations adhere to the Associated Students of the University of Arizona (ASUA) guidelines. Our bylaws emphasize inclusivity, integrity, and respect among members, ensuring a supportive and enjoyable chess community for all.

For more details on the ASUA bylaws, please visit the ASUA Clubs Handbook website.
